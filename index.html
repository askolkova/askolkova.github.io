Alena Skolkova

I am a Ph.D. candidate at CERGE-EI (Prague). My general fields of interest are econometric theory and applied machine learning. Broadly speaking, I am interested in application of machine learning methods for improving efficiency of traditional econometric methods, i.e. providing extra robustness to the data irregularities that are frequently encountered in practice.

For example, in my Job Market Paper, I show how the fnite-sample properties of model averaging estimators can be improved via ridge regularization.
