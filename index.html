Alena Skolkova

I am a Ph.D. candidate at CERGE-EI (Prague). My general fields of interest are econometric theory and machine learning. My research focuses on high-dimensional methods for causal inference and prediction problems. In particular, I aim to improve micro-econometric methods for causal analysis via machine learning. I am also interested in applications of machine learning (especially natural language processing) to macroeconomic prediction problems.

For example, in my Job Market Paper, I show how model averaging can be improved by adding ridge regularization. Visit this page to learn about my other papers.

You can find my CV here.
