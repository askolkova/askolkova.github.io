Alena Skolkova

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher in Data Science at the Department of Economics, Faculty of Law at the Charles University in Prague.

My general fields of interest are econometric theory and applied machine learning. My research focuses on high-dimensional methods for causal inference and prediction problems. I am also interested in applications of machine learning (especially natural language processing) to macroeconomic prediction problems. You can find my CV here.
